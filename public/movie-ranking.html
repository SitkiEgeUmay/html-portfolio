The Best Movies According to Ege

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Prestige

This movie is truly my number one. It's a movie that really makes you think, and every detail, from the plot to the actors, was magnificent.

Ayla

This movie was truly emotional. It has a great significance for us Turks. It's impossible not to cry.

The Fast and Furious Series

This movie is really good in terms of action. Yes, the events are unrealistic and this is too much for you to believe, but it's a hit for car enthusiasts.
